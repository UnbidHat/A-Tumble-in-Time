# Casting
**Casting Time** 1 standard action
**Components** V, S

# Effect
**Range** close (25 ft. + 5 ft./2 levels)
**Effect** 15-ft.-radius spherical projection of night sky
**Duration** concentration + 3 rounds
**Saving Throw** none; **Spell Resistance** Will disbelief (harmless)

# Description
You project an image of the night sky based on your current location and the local time, allowing you to observe the heavens and all of its celestial bodies and features even during the daytime, indoors, or underground.
